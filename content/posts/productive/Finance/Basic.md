# Finance

Finance is the study of management,movement, and raising of money. The 'Finance' can be used as a *verb*, such as First National Bank agrees to finance your home mortage loan. At its essence, the study of finance is about understanding the uses and sources of cash. 

- It can be also be used as a noun referring to an entire industry.
- Finance can also be a tool to make us better at decision making.
