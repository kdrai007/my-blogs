# Investment

Investments are product and processes used to create and grow wealth. Most commonly investment includes the discussion and application of different types of financial instruments, delivery vehicles, regulation, risk-and-return opportunities.Topics also include a discussion of stocks,bonds and derivative securities such as futures and options. 

- A broad coverage of investment instruments include mutual funds, exchange traded funds(ETFs), and investment vehicles such as 401k plans or individual retirement accounts(IRAs).
