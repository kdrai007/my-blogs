# Recognize Sale or Expanse

Most large companies use accrual accounting because it gives more accurate picture of their real financial performance. The recognition of sale(Revenue) and expanses is governed by two core principals that keep everything organized, regardless of when cash is actually exchanged.

### Recognizing the Sale (Revenue)

A sale, or revenue is recognized based on the **Revenue recognition principal**. The rule is simple: Revenue is **recognized when it is earned, not when the cash is received**.

##### How is revenue "Earned"?

Revenue is the value of goods and services the organization sold or provided to customers for a given period of time. In accounting, **revenue recognition** involves recording, sales or fees earned within a period earned. 



----

Deferred Revenue: Also known as unearned revenue, is money company receives in advance for goods or services that are yet to delivered.
