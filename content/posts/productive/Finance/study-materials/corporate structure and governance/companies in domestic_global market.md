# Companies in Global and Domestic Markets. 

If a company become global or multinational in scope, fundamental analysis of the organization by investment community become more complex. In order to better understand a company, it is important to determine what laws affect the company's governance and which set of accounting rules used to fashion its financial reports.

Domestic company operates within or for the most part within the boarders of country. While such organizations import raw material from outside of country or export finished product to other countries, in the end these international activities shows only a small portion of their overall business activities.
