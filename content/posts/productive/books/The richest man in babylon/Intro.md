> "The richest man of babylon" is written by *Georage S. Classon*

So, This is books is about **Babylon** an ancient city originated at *Mesopotamia*. And how this city becomes the weathiest city in the world.

### Glossary

1. Euphrates -> River in Babylon.
2. Sumerites -> Inhabitants of Babylon.
3. Tigris -> River in Babylon.
4. Herodotus -> Greek traveler  and Historian.
5. Bansir -> Chariot builder.
6. Kobbi -> Best friend with bansir, and a musician
7. Arkad -> Richest man in Babylon, Old friends of Kobbi and Bansir.
