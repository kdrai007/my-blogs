# The Phrase and The Clause

1. A group of words and make sense, but not complete sense is called `phrase`.

Example,

The sun rises `in the east`.
There came a giant `to my door`.
Show me `how to do it`.

2. A group of words that which forms part of a sentence, and contains a Subject and a Predicate, is called  a `Clause`.

Example,

People `who pay their debt` are trusted.
We cannot start `while it is raining`.
I think that `you have made a mistake`.
