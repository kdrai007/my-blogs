Sentence

A group of words, which make complete sense is called sentence.

Kind of sentences

Sentences are of four kinds :-

  1. Those which make statements or assertions; as,

Humpty Dumpty sat on a wall

  1. Those which ask questions; as

Where do you live ?

  1. Those which express commands, requests, or entreaties; as,

Be quiet. Have mercy on us.

  1. Those which express strong feelings; as,

How cold the night is! What a shame!
